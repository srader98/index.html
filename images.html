Images

#1

For both images I wanted to create a sense of vacancy and loneliness.
With image one, I took the photo with the idea of a using the
diagonal staircase for composition. I went with a warm, darker
platters adding a bit of noise to create a vintage look. Using the
exposure effect and playing with a slight offset. This on top of
changing the color with hue and saturation as well as fixing the shadows and highlights with the curves tool.

#2

Again with both images I wanted the theme of vacancy and loneliness.
This sense a stillness that will eventually be filled. With Image 2, I
did my best to follow the rule of thirds and keep my main subject in
the lower right corners. I focuses a more cooler overall color while
highlighting the single red seat in the middle of the image.
With that I used the color balance and vibrance tools respectively. I used the exposure to empathize the shadows in the image.
